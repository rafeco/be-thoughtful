Be Thoughtful

A CRM to make the holidays a year-round project

About This App

Be Thoughtful is a lightweight, local-first web application that turns Christmas gift-giving and card-sending into a year-round project. Think of it as a "CRM for staying thoughtful" - it tracks people, captures gift ideas throughout the year, manages card preferences, and guides you through the September-December workflow with appropriate prompts and deadlines.

No cloud services, no subscriptions, no complexity. Just a simple tool to help you capture ideas in January, plan in the summer, and execute smoothly when the season arrives.

Key Features
  • People Management - Track family, friends, colleagues, and contacts
  • Gift Ideas - Capture ideas year-round, not just in December
  • Card Tracking - Manage handwritten cards and e-cards
  • Timeline View - Monthly guidance from September through December
  • Milestone Subtasks - Break down each month into trackable tasks
  • Year Rollover - Automatic archiving and planning for next year
  • Pre-planning Phase - Helpful suggestions during January-August
  • AI Assistant Integration - Copy prompts for Claude/ChatGPT to help brainstorm
Technology

Built with:

  • Python Flask for the backend
  • SQLite for local data storage
  • Bootstrap 5 for the UI
  • Festive Christmas color scheme

Open source project - View on GitHub

Philosophy

The best gift-giving comes from thoughtfulness, not last-minute panic. This app helps you capture ideas throughout the year, plan ahead, and execute smoothly when the season arrives. By spreading the work across several months and keeping a record of what worked (and what didn't), you can make each year better than the last.
